# Community Code of Conduct

AgentCube follows the [CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md).

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

For the full policy text, see the CNCF Code of Conduct linked above.
